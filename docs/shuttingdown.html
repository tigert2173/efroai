Why EFROAI is Shutting Down

After much thought and reflection, I’ve made the difficult decision to shut down EFROAI. While this may mark the end of one chapter, it also opens the door to a new direction for my work.

EFROAI was created to give users the freedom to explore, and while that intention was sincere, over time it became clear that the platform’s unrestricted access to certain types of content could lead to harmful, unintended consequences. What began as a way to support niche interests soon revealed that such freedom could contribute to mental health struggles, strained relationships, and other negative impacts that I can no longer ignore.

As a developer, I feel a strong responsibility to ensure that the tools I help create contribute to the greater good, rather than enabling potential harm. I would hate for something I helped build to cause damage to people’s mental health, personal lives, or relationships. This realization is what led me to decide that EFROAI should no longer be maintained.

If any of you have been impacted by the content on EFROAI, whether emotionally, mentally, or personally, I want to encourage you to take the necessary steps to prioritize your well-being. It’s important to remember that your mental health is a priority, and you don’t have to face these challenges alone.

In the process of developing EFROAI, I also provided valuable insights and support to BotBridgeAI, a platform focused on providing decentralized free AI to 3rd-party developers and backends for developers to use in their sites. They will be releasing a platform character hub soon that is for bots that assist with things like technology help, studying, and productivity–you may find this cool to check out! While EFROAI is shutting down, my contributions have helped shape BotBridgeAI’s development, and I hope this company can find success in their future.

While this is a difficult change, it marks a shift towards a new direction, one that aligns more closely with the kind of work I feel good about contributing to. Thank you to everyone who supported EFROAI, and I hope you’ll continue to join me as we support and help shape the future of BotBridgeAI.

EFROAI Shutdown Date: Jan 20th, 2025
