Guidelines for Slots 1, 2, and 3:

Guidelines for Other Slots:

These slots can feature more mature themes but must not include full NSFW content. Content can include partial nudity or sensitive themes but should not be graphic or explicit.

Specific Restrictions:

Full frontal nudity is not allowed. However, nudity from behind (such as showing the backside) is acceptable as long as it remains in the realm of light NSFW content, meaning it should be mild and not explicit or sexualized.

Additional Notes:

No real people images are permitted in any of the slots. All images should be of either fictional characters, illustrations, or abstract/artistic representations.
