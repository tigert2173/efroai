General Conduct

All users are expected to behave respectfully towards others, promoting a welcoming and friendly atmosphere. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying will not be tolerated.

Content Guidelines

Appropriate Content:

All content shared must be suitable for the community. Offensive, hateful, or explicit material is strictly prohibited. Explicit material includes images depicting sexual actions and will not be tolerated to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all users.

Attribution:

When sharing others' work, always give proper credit to the original creator.

Character Images:

Character images must be restricted to undergarments at the most. Any fully nude images will be removed to maintain a clean page and promote the mental health of our team and community members.

In the future, we plan to introduce a feature that allows additional character images, including explicit ones. These images will be blurred by default and will only be viewable if the user opts to display explicit content.

Important: Character images must not depict real people. Only fictional characters are allowed to ensure privacy and respect for individuals.

Posting Guidelines:

When posting chatbots, please include the fetishes they contain in your post. While there are no consequences for not tagging fetishes, other users will appreciate being able to more easily identify the content of your post.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed:

  • Spamming or excessive self-promotion
  • Sharing of personal information without consent
  • Using multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Creating characters that deceptively resemble real people
  • Creating bots representing minors on our platform
  • Using our platform as a minor
  • Beastiality, aka. creating bots/characters that represent bipedal or quadrupedal animals to be used for sexual intentions.

Enforcement

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community, depending on the severity of the action.

Reporting Issues

If you notice any violations of these rules, please report them to the moderators at report@efroai.net.
